# Changelog

## 1.0.0 — 2026-06-26

Initial public release.

- Procedural skybox: atmosphere gradient, animated sun + glow, **phase-lit textured moon**, procedural point-stars and a baked Milky Way cubemap.
- **Volumetric raymarched clouds** — curved planet-shell model, baked 3D Worley/FBM noise, Beer–Lambert + Henyey–Greenstein + powder lighting, moon-lit at night.
- **9 weather presets** with smooth crossfades: Clear, Partly Cloudy, Overcast, Light Rain, Rain, Storm, Light Snow, Blizzard, Fog.
- **Raymarched aurora** — 3D curtains with ribbon contours + vertical rays, multi-colour gradient, on random nights, fading in/out, weaving through the clouds.
- Player-following rain/snow (whole-world coverage) plus a localized `FixedArea` zone mode; wind-driven slant.
- Lightning flashes during storms; in-shader sky fog.
- **Multiplayer-synced** time / weather / aurora via a shared UTC clock — no networking.
- Custom editor control panel: time-of-day scrubber + one-click weather buttons, live edit-mode preview.
- All textures generated by the included procedural baker (original assets, MIT-safe).
- Demo scene included.
